# Surveillance

The modern life takes place in an environment where a lot of data is collected and processed. When people interact with technical devices, browse in the web, have their cellphones connected to the internet or get recorded by public surveillance cameras, the produced data contains information about them and their lives.
The whistleblower [Edward Snowden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Snowden) has leaked information about programs of mass collection of these data that are produced [(Interview)](https://archive.org/details/SnowdenInterviewInEnglishARD480p).
